Professional Summary

Associate Professor at Xidian University (School of Telecommunications Engineering). My research focuses on multimedia communication and quality assessment in general, including but not limited to coding and transmission, immersive media compression (avatars/animation streams), 3D Gaussian Splatting (3DGS) evaluation and quality metrics and Generative Face Video Coding (GFVC). I am actively involved in ISO/IEC standardization (MPEG and JVET), with technical leadership roles in animation compression, MPEG-I Part 39 (Avatar Representation Format), and Gaussian Splat Coding. I have been elected as a member of of theMultimedia Systems and Applications Technical Committee (MSA-TC) and the Visual Signal Processing and Communications Technical Committee (VSPC-TC) of the IEEE Circuits and Systems Society (CAS) as well as elected as a member of theMultimedia Signal Processing Technical Committee (MMSP-TC) of the IEEE Signal Processing Society (SPS).
